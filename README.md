# DS Virtual Pet
This is a simple virtual pet game me and [BowersIndustry](https://github.com/BowersIndustry) are making.

# What do you do?
In this game, you feed, and battle your pet to level them up!

# Planned Features
- Feeding Your Pet
- Playing With Your Pet
- Leveling Up Your Pet
- Offline Battling
- Local Battling
- Online Battling
